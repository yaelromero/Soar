"Soar"

A Story of Adventure

"Soar" is an animation in the first person point of view. The character in the animation runs at the beginning and then leaps into the air to begin flying. Then, the character looks down and has a bird's eye view of the city below.

Target Audience

Ages 13+, teens and adults who admire landscapes, travel, and fantasy.
